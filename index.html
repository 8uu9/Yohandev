How long does a typical project take? +

Most websites take 2–4 weeks. Full apps usually run 6–12 weeks depending on scope. I share a clear timeline before we start.

Do you work with teams or just solo founders? +

Both. I've shipped solo for early-stage founders and joined existing teams as a senior engineer. Whatever the setup needs.

Can you take over an existing codebase? +

Yes — I love refactors. I'll start with a quick audit, agree on priorities, then ship improvements without breaking things.

Do you offer post-launch support? +

Always. Every project includes a 30-day support window, and I offer monthly retainers for ongoing work.
